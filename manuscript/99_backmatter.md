The Demographic and Health Survey Program protocols are reviewed and approved by the ICF Institutional Review Board (Rockville, MD, USA) and the Nigeria National Health Research Ethics Committee. The 2024 Nigeria DHS was conducted under these approvals. Secondary analysis of the publicly available, de-identified DHS dataset did not require additional ethical review under ICF DHS terms of use.
